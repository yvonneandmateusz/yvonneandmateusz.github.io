On the day

The ceremony will be held on June 17 th , 2017 at 4:00pm sharp at the St Mary’s Basilica, known to locals as Mariacki.
We are lucky to be wed at such a beautiful and historic church that has been conducting weddings since the 1500s. You can learn more about the church here. Guests are welcome to arrive early and enjoy the beauty of the main square in Krakow, which is one of the largest medieval town squares in Europe. A coach to the main square is provided for those staying at the Palace and a coach will be provided after the ceremony to shuttle guests to the reception. Our reception will be taking place at 6:00pm at the Paszkowka Palace, a nineteenth-century neo-gothic palace 40 minutes outside of Krakow. Honouring the Polish tradition of partying late, the reception will take place from 6:00pm to 4:00am and plenty of vodka will be served at the open bar. We have planned a full night of entertainment for our guests including a Chinese lion dance, a magician, a photo booth, wedding games and obnoxious amounts of dancing. If you have any questions please contact us.

Lodging

The palace has a hotel on site and there are a limited amount of beds that we can allocate to our guests for June 16th, 17th 2017. The cost is $40 CAD a night per person. If you are interested in staying on site, please let us know. Otherwise, there are a variety of hotels in Krakow city center, which is only about a 30 minute cab ride away from the venue. We understand this may be a lot of information, especially when planning travel abroad but we assure you it is very straightforward. If you have any questions please contact us

Getting around

Most guests will be arriving at the airport in Krakow, Balice, KRK. There is a train (20 minutes) and a bus (30 minutes) to the city center from the airport. For those coming to the Palace, we advise that you take a cab from the airport. Transportation during the wedding will be organized and we will have a coach that will take people from the Palace to the church and back.
For guests from Toronto there is a direct flight from Toronto to Warsaw (WAW) by LOT Polish airlines. Warsaw is Poland capital and worth visiting for its own merits, train from Warsaw to Krakow takes 2h. Our initial research showed that route seems to be the cheapest. If you plan to visit multiple locations in Europe we suggest trying to buy a multi-city ticket as sometimes they can be cheaper than direct flights. If you have any questions please contact us
